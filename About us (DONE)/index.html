Our History

NAWRAS AL RAIDA started in Iraq on behalf of the Al-bani company in 1995 as a small fabrication yard servicing the Oil & Gas and Power sectors in Iraq

  • Beginning
    The small yard’s activities and scope expanded to cover more activities and more sectors of the industry. At the beginning of the second millennium, NAWRAS AL RAIDA had already moved to a new larger location in the Sultanate of Oman equipped with better equipment and technologies. At the same time, we established our offices in Jordan and Singapore to serve our activities in the Sultanate of Oman and other countries in the middle east, and in East Asia and north African regions
  • Presernt
    Better equipped, NAWRAS AL RAIDA continues to date to venture and reap its reward by participating in medium to large-scale projects. Domestic projects with international clientele have opened new areas of developments. Focus in marketing strategy was redirected towards project specific specialization
  • Achivements
    A broader and global scope is now conceived and growing demand in the international market has led NAWRAS AL RAIDA to establish several offices in the region. Currently all our fabrication works are being carried out at our new production facility in Oman and USA. The facility has been equipped to handle fabrication for all types of projects servicing our markets
  • Future
    We are focused on creating innovative products and solutions. We are constantly expanding the list of offered products to meet the goals of our clients and their individual needs
